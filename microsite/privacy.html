2. How we use it

To provide and bill the service, prevent abuse, keep it reliable, and contact you about your account. We do not use your submitted structures to build or sell datasets.

3. Your molecular structures

Structures you submit are processed to return results. We cache derived results (keyed by a structure hash) for speed; async-job inputs/outputs are stored temporarily for download, then deleted. We do not sell, license, or share your submitted structures.

4. Cookies & advertising

This website uses a cookie to remember your consent choice and for basic analytics. If you accept on the cookie banner, we may show ads via Google AdSense, which uses cookies/identifiers to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. If you decline, no advertising scripts are loaded. You can change your choice by clearing site data. Learn more at Google's advertising policies, and opt out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings. The Android app does not show ads unless stated in its store listing.

5. Sharing / third parties

Service providers only: a cloud host, Stripe (payments), Google Play Billing (Android), an email provider, and — if you consent — Google AdSense. We do not sell personal data.

6. Your rights

Access, export, rotate your key, or delete your account and data anytime — in the app (Account → Delete), via DELETE /account, or by emailing privacy@photon-bounce.com. Depending on your location (GDPR/CCPA) you may have additional rights.

7. Security, retention & children

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and kept only as long as needed. Q-Mol is a professional/research tool not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes

We'll update this page and the date above when practices change.
