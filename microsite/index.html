Everything a chemist needs from a structure

One SMILES in, a full property panel out — backed by RDKit.

Descriptors

The full ~217 RDKit 2D panel — MW, logP, TPSA, QED, and more, or a named subset.

Fingerprints

Morgan/ECFP, MACCS, RDKit, AtomPair, Torsion — as on-bits or base64.

Similarity & clustering

Pairwise Tanimoto matrices, Butina clustering, MCS, and diversity picks.

Drug-likeness & ADMET

Lipinski/Veber/PAINS screening plus logS/BBB/hERG/GI heuristics.

3D & shape

Conformers and shape descriptors (NPR1/NPR2, radius of gyration).

Identifiers

Canonical SMILES, InChI, InChIKey, MolBlock — and de-duplication.
