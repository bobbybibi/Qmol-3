# Photon Bounce — Q-Mol SaaS Telemarketing Plan

## 1. Target Audience
- **Pharma R&D Directors** (small/mid/large pharma, biotech)
- **Venture Capital/Investors** (life sciences, SaaS, AI/ML, digital health)
- **Innovation/IT Managers** (pharma, CROs, chemical suppliers)

## 2. Outreach Channels
- **Phone:** Direct calls to company main lines, ask for R&D, innovation, or IT decision makers
- **Email:** Personalized cold emails to R&D, innovation, or investment contacts (LinkedIn, company sites, Crunchbase)
- **LinkedIn:** Connection + intro message

## 3. Call Script (Pharma)
---
Hello, this is [Telemarketer Name] calling on behalf of Photon Bounce. We’re the developers behind Q-Mol, a next-generation cheminformatics SaaS platform for molecular design, screening, and retrosynthesis. 

We’re reaching out to select pharma teams to offer early access and partnership. We have two engagement models:

1. **Co-build:** You fund the build, get a custom solution, and we take a small equity stake.
2. **Deploy-first:** We build at our cost, you pay on deployment, and we share a larger equity/profit split.

Would you be the right person to discuss digital R&D tools, or can you connect me with your innovation lead?
